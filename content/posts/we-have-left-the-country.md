# 04 — We have left the country

*21 November 2017*

Quick update — we are now air side at Brisbane International Airport. On the very good advice of Brad and Kris, we have pre-purchased all sorts of alcoholic delights to pick up from customs when we return. I'm giving Wild Turkey American Honey a try, and Nicole is trying out an upmarket brand of Baileys! Plus some good old Bonded Jim Beam.

About to board for our short haul flight to Auckland. I hope that long white cloud doesn't stop us landing. Will post again soon …

# Knights Around the World — USA, Germany & UK, 2017–18

> *Around the world in 53 days and back to Rocky*

Steve, Nicole and Katelyn Knight left Rockhampton on 21 November 2017 and returned in January 2018, covering the USA (west coast, national parks, Minneapolis, Washington DC, New York), Germany (Frankfurt, Heidelberg, Rothenburg, Nuremberg, Munich) and the UK (Malvern, Oxford, Bristol, Bath, London).

Originally published at [knightsaroundtheworld.wordpress.com](https://knightsaroundtheworld.wordpress.com).

# 02 — Well, it's been a busy few days!

*21 November 2017*

![903eb5b4-7470-4d84-9de1-7085000919b8.jpeg](/assets/903eb5b4-7470-4d84-9de1-7085000919b8.jpeg)

Our lovely housesitters, Bruce and Di, arrived yesterday. Bruce did a fantastic job backing their van in, a task made a lot more complicated by the rubbish skip sitting on the front lawn that the builder replacing our deck has there currently. But all's well that ends well, and so we had a really nice BBQ dinner last night with Bruce and Di and our good friends from up the street, Brad and Kris.

Our last meal at home for quite a while!

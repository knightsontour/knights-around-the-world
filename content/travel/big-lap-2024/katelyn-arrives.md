*26 May 2024*

Very early in the morning after Heli Fish (around 1:00am!), our daughter Katelyn arrived to spend a week with us. We had been so looking forward to seeing her, and so it was great to finally have her here in Darwin with us.

In the caravan park we’re staying at, we were able to get a studio cabin and adjoining powered site so that we were literally back-to-back. This made it very easy to stay in touch during the week, while still giving Katelyn some space.

We put together a plan for the week, which kept us pretty busy. That’s partly why these blog posts have dried up a little over the last week, but you’ll get to hear about it through the blog posts and Map section.

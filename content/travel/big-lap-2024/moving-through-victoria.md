*20 December 2024*

Nicole and I are [moving through Victoria](https://knightsj.travelmap.net) reasonably quickly. After traversing the Great Ocean Road (in a day), and a lovely stay in Geelong —including meeting relatives I hadn’t met before (thanks again for your hospitality Gordon and Anne) — we’ve started exploring Eastern Victoria.

After a wonderful ferry trip from Queenscliff to Sorrento, we had a fun half day or so exploring the Mornington Peninsula. We had a phenomenally good weather day, putting the Mornington Peninsula in its best outfit. And, while it’s very beautiful in that outfit, I couldn’t help thinking about how much money she has around her. Beautiful houses, beautiful people (present company included), stunning views, and luxury cars. Good for them I suppose.

We’ve now moved into the Gippsland region, currently staying in the quaint country town of Yarram. We’ll be visiting Wilsons Promontory tomorrow, which should be great.

And while Brisbane suffers through relentless storms, rain and flooding, the weather is bloody brilliant down here.

We’re looking forward to Christmas in Melbourne, and not sure what weather Santa will bring for us. Until then …

*24 March 2024*

Nicole and I had been trying to come up with a nickname for our motorhome once we’d bought it. Back in the early 2000s we’d named our “tinny” *Knight Moves* (for the Bob Seger fans out there), and we didn’t want to recycle that one.

I can remember watching a certain 80s TV show back in late primary school starring The Hoff (before he was The Hoff). The show centred around a black [Pontiac Firebird Trans Am](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Third-generation_Pontiac_Firebird) T-top called [K.I.T.T. (Knight Industries Two Thousand)](https://knight-rider.fandom.com/wiki/K.I.T.T._(2000)#:~:text=By%20the%20time%20of%20the,his%201957%20Chevrolet%20Bel%20Air.) with AI capabilities, allowing K.I.T.T. to converse with The Hoff and help him solve crimes. These days it would probably be Ryan Gosling in a (probably pink) Tesla.

![image_1742030792_714455599.webp](/assets/image_1742030792_714455599.webp)

So, we went with that and have named our Explorer Spirit motorhome *Knight Rider**s*** because, while there was (and thankfully only ever will be) one Hoff, there’s two of us.  
Fortunately, a very dear and long-time friend of ours (thanks Andrew!) is a talented sign writer and was able to arrange for us a fantastic retro logo. Thanks Andrew!

*3 May 2024*

Nicole and I chose an “easy lunch” option in Katherine before heading to Katherine Gorge. After our third trip to the Katherine Woolworths and spying a Brumby’s Bakery right out the front each time, I relented and bought a sausage roll. For full disclosure, Nicole bought a vegetable pastie.

We couldn’t eat them there or outside as there was nowhere in the shade to sit (VERY hot here still), so we went for a short drive to the local sporting grounds and watched the sporting oval getting mowed while we ate.

Verdict? *A bit on the thin side. Acceptable texture. Shallow on taste. Did the job, but nothing special. 7.2.*

**Current scorecard**

- Roma (8.5/10)
- Katherine (7.2/10)
- Cloncurry (7.0/10)
- Winton (6.5/10)

![image_1742030796_1373511823.webp](/assets/image_1742030796_1373511823.webp)

Let’s hope things improve in Kakadu or Darwin.

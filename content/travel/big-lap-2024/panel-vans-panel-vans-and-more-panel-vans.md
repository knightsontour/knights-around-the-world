*1 April 2024*

We shared Top of the Town Tourist Park in Stanthorpe (and the neighbouring Showgrounds) with the [49th Australian Van Nats](https://www.vannationals.com.au/). The “Nats” were set up in the Showgrounds, however a lot of competitors were staying in the caravan park. A slice of Aussie history living on through the hard work and dedication of these enthusiasts. And didn’t they look and sound fantastic!

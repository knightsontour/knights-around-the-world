Back in 2012 we had planned a trip around Australia in our caravan but didn’t quite get there as things changed. Now, we have another chance to do it in our trusty motorhome “Knight Riders” and we couldn’t be more excited. A big focus for us this trip will be slowing down and taking in all this great country has to offer.

We’re taking a mid-life Gap Year (well, nearly a year) to drive our Explorer motorhome, Knight Riders, around Australia. We’ll be spending a lot of time in the NT, WA and SA, and then see how we’re going after that. It will be a time to see what we haven’t seen, meet a lot of people, eat, drink, write and photograph. And, most importantly, slow down.

*29 April 2024*

You may have noticed that I’ve been having some issues with images displaying on blog posts — specifically that they won’t. I spent around 2 hours yesterday and 3 hours this morning trying to fix it and couldn’t. Very frustrating!

![image_1742030796_1247611293.webp](/assets/image_1742030796_1247611293.webp)

Then I had an after-lunch nap and Nicole said she would have a look at it. Nicole dabbled in setting up a side business building websites while we were living in Rockhampton and, like anything Nicole puts her mind to, she became very good at it.

Anyway, when I woke from my nap Nicole had debugged the website, identified the issue, found a solution and got it working. Thanks very much Nicole, and I definitely know who to turn to next time!

We will be heading into places like Katherine Gorge and Kakadu over the next week or two, so it’s good to know I can share the photos with you.

PS You will have noticed a new look and feel on the website. I hope you like it.

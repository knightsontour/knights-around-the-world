*19 October 2024*

*Sausage Roll Scorecard* updates have fallen into a state of disrepair of late and Greg J, I know you miss them. I have **seven** updates to catch up on now, so this update will be low on storytelling and high on brevity.

And, for the record, as of yesterday’s *Friday Weigh-in* I’m somehow still under the weight that I left home at. Go figure …

### Wild Poppy Cafe (Merredin)

- Different pastry — quite nice.
- Different filling including carrots — quite tasty.
- Nice and hot.
- Nicole really liked it — so extra 0.2 points.
- Rating — 8.5 out of 10.

![image_1742030847_668821210.webp](/assets/image_1742030847_668821210.webp)

### Forrest French Hot Bread (Collie)

- Vietnamese staff — so I felt very positive.
- Small in size.
- Not that hot.
- Tasted OK but nothing special.
- Rating — 7.9 out of 10.

![image_1742030847_1806276963.webp](/assets/image_1742030847_1806276963.webp)

### Best in the West Bakery (Bunbury)

- We were getting groceries on our way to Myalup when Nicole spotted the bakery and we both said, “Why not!”
- Nice presentation and size.
- Good temperature.
- Filling quite dense but still tasty.
- Rating — 8.4 out of 10.

![image_1742030847_237076022.webp](/assets/image_1742030847_237076022.webp)

![image_1742030847_664736889.webp](/assets/image_1742030847_664736889.webp)

### Wave Rock Cafe

- Very different appearance.
- Homemade — very nice pastry.
- Quite greasy — a good thing.
- Nice taste — with veges inside.
- Very impressive.
- Rating — 8.9 out of 10 (going Top 4!)

![image_1742030847_1467132613.webp](/assets/image_1742030847_1467132613.webp)

![image_1742030848_486159095.webp](/assets/image_1742030848_486159095.webp)

### Ceduna Bakery (Ceduna)

- Nice and long!
- Good temperature.
- OK taste.
- Rating — 8.2 out of 10.

![image_1742030848_1395745363.webp](/assets/image_1742030848_1395745363.webp)

### Elliott’s Bakery & Cafe (Streaky Bay)

- Nice town and nice bakery.
- Friendly service.
- Split it with Nicole — so as not to spoil lunch later that day.
- A different appearance, but good taste.
- Rating — 8.3 out of 10.

![image_1742030848_763946382.webp](/assets/image_1742030848_763946382.webp)

### Elliston Bakery (Elliston)

- Looks good — and long!
- A bit too hot.
- Nice crunchy pastry.
- Greasy taste — a good thing.
- Firm filling — not really my style.
- Overall, pretty good.
- Rating — 8.2 out of 10.

![image_1742030848_1543986330.webp](/assets/image_1742030848_1543986330.webp)

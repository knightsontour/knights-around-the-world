*22 November 2017*

Just arrived in Auckland. Wonderful flight with great seats and plenty of movies to choose from. Food was nice too, so A+ for Air NZ so far.

The real test is about to come for them though, with a 12 hour long haul to San Francisco. I'm sure they'll be up to the challenge.
